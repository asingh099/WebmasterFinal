Process

Farm-Table

All our produce is sourced from local farms which allows us to support local businesses, and build the economy. This decreases our carbon footprint due to shorter food transportation distances. Farms utilize eco-friendly methods and practices of raising and cultivating crops and livestock. The quick transportation times decrease the need for preservatives, resulting in healthier dishes for our customers.

Preparation

All food is prepared in a timely fashion from when it is received, ensuring fresh food for our customers. Produce is carefully washed and the water is passed into our greywater system, thoroughly filtering it for another use. Additionally, our use of energy-efficient appliances, such as stoves and dishwashers, greatly decreases our carbon footprint. All packaging used in the store is biodegradable, allowing it for use in our compost system where the fertilizer produced is used for our many small gardens scattered throughout and outside the restaurant.

Our Mission

"At Viva Natura, our mission is to create delicious, plant-based meals that celebrate the natural bounty of the earth. We are committed to sourcing fresh, seasonal ingredients directly from local farms, ensuring that every dish we serve is both nourishing and sustainable. By embracing eco-friendly practices and supporting our community of growers, we aim to make mindful eating accessible and enjoyable for all. Our passion is rooted in promoting a healthier planet, one meal at a time."
